International Conference on Contemporary Issues in Accounting & Finance (ICCIAF-2024)

September 13-14, 2024 (Hybrid Mode)

Organized by the Master of Finance and Control Programme, Department of Accountancy and Business Statistics, University College of Commerce and Management Studies, Mohanlal Sukhadia University, Udaipur & Indian Accounting Association, Udaipur Branch

Conference Objective

The Department of Accountancy and Business Statistics is pleased to announce the International Conference on Contemporary Issues in Accounting and Finance. This conference aims to discuss and update the delegates about the rapid transformations in accounting and finance through technologies like AI, blockchain, and more. The conference will create an interface among professionals, academicians, and experts in the field of accounting education and finance.

Call for Papers

Academicians, research scholars, practitioners, executives, and students are invited to participate in the conference by contributing research papers. The guidelines for paper submission are as follows:

  • Original and unpublished work is sought.
  • Papers must be based on conference themes and sub-themes.
  • Length: 3,000 to 5,000 words.
  • Title: Maximum 15 words.
  • Abstract: Maximum 300 words.
  • Format: Times New Roman, 12pt, 1.5 spacing, A4 paper layout.
